ABOUT MENTALLY FRIENDLY

Mentally Friendly is an agile digital product studio that strives to create meaningful and engaging experiences that solve real needs. We’re made up of fifty passionate team members split across two studios in Sydney and London.

We’re a friendly bunch (its in the name), so a social personality, sense of humor, party tricks and a good play list will go a long way. We host regular knowledge sharing events and enjoy an annual holiday away together every year.

We are only as strong as the people who work here. We’re serious about agitating the digital space, working collaboratively in the pursuit of awesomeness and enjoying ourselves along the way.
